Our Story

Think about the "Boomers" who have no understanding of technology and new trends, our uncle was among these boomers. One day he had a dream. People in his dreams said that because of the people who are ignorant to technological development or who have difficulty in perceiving these developments, the end is approaching, and the only person who can save the world is our uncle. Because of that, they loaded a comprehensive program in his memory so that he can perceive and use new technologies. The main task of our uncle is to ensure that all other boomers can keep up with the new age, and then to establish a colony on the Moon with these Boomers who keep up with the times.

8888 Boomer characters who can keep up with the times will be able to join this colony . Among these Boomer, we will have 88 special Boomer characters, and people who have NFTs of these characters will have special powers and have the chance to win surprise prizes in the "Moon colonization" themed game in the future.
